Welcome to the list. You'll start receiving email updates from this site, and here's what to expect.

When a new article is published, you'll get an email that Thursday morning with a link to read it. On weeks where nothing new goes up, you won't hear from me at all — no filler, no fluff, just new content when it's ready. Most weeks that means one article, and sometimes it means a quiet week with nothing.

That's it. Simple and infrequent by design.
